Take Out Style Rotisserie Chicken

Love that rotisserie chicken you get at the store? Well, this is even better because you make it fresh, season it to your liking, and you get to watch it cook. Maybe we should call it "Better Than Take Out Style Rotisserie Chicken."

20

Prep Time

45

Cooking Time

4-6

Serves

Ingredients
Ingredient Qty
Whole Fryer Chicken1 Unit
Olive Oil1/2 Cup
Honey2 Tbsp
Lime Juice2 Tbsp
Sea Salt2 Tsp
Paprika1/2 Tsp
Black Pepper1/2 Tsp
Instructions
  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Clean and prepare chicken for rotisserie.
  3. Combine remaining ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat. Mix until honey is completely melted and mixture is smooth.
  4. Remove from heat and cool for 10 minutes.
  5. Baste chicken with sauce and place on rotisserie on preheated grill.
  6. Cook for about 45 to 50 minutes at a temperature around 400 F, basting occasionally with sauce.
  7. When done (internal temperature of 165 F), remove from grill and wrap tightly in foil. Let sit for about 15 minutes.
  8. Then carve, serve, and enjoy!
